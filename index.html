Who We Are

A Lab Built for the Future of Knowledge

Learnix Research Lab is a forward-thinking research group dedicated to advancing the science of learning through rigorous inquiry, cutting-edge technology, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.

🔬

Rigorous Research

Evidence-based methodology and peer-reviewed output across all domains.

🤝

Collaboration

Partnering with universities, industry, and government worldwide.

💡

Innovation

Translating research into real-world solutions that make an impact.

🎓

Education

Mentoring the next generation of researchers and thinkers.

🎯 Our Mission & Vision

To advance human understanding through innovative research at the intersection of learning science and artificial intelligence.
To develop intelligent systems that augment human capabilities and democratize access to knowledge globally.
To foster an inclusive, collaborative, and ethics-first research culture that benefits society at large.
To bridge the gap between foundational research and practical applications in education, healthcare, and industry.
What We Study

Research Areas

Our work spans six core domains, each connecting the science of learning with the frontiers of intelligent systems.

🤖

Machine Learning & AI

Developing novel ML algorithms, deep learning architectures, and scalable AI systems for intelligent decision-making and prediction.

Deep Learning Reinforcement Learning AutoML
📚

Natural Language Processing

Advancing NLP methods for text understanding, semantic analysis, language generation, and multilingual processing.

LLMs Sentiment Analysis NER
🧠

Cognitive Science

Exploring how humans learn, remember, and reason — bridging psychology, neuroscience, and AI to understand intelligence.

Memory Reasoning Metacognition
📊

Data Science & Analytics

Turning raw data into actionable insight through statistical modeling, visualization, and advanced analytics pipelines.

Predictive Modeling Data Mining Viz
🎓

Educational Technology

Designing intelligent tutoring systems, adaptive learning platforms, and tools that personalize education at scale.

Adaptive Learning ITS EdTech
⚖️

AI Ethics & Fairness

Ensuring AI systems are transparent, equitable, and aligned with human values — from bias detection to explainability.

Bias Mitigation Explainability Safety

Shape the Future of Learning Science

We're looking for curious, driven individuals — from undergraduates to postdocs — ready to tackle hard problems and make a real impact.

Learnix Research Lab is a growing, welcoming environment where ideas are valued, collaboration is encouraged, and every member contributes meaningfully to our research mission.

We accept applications on a rolling basis for most positions. Funded opportunities are competitive and reviewed each semester. Don't see the right fit? Reach out anyway — we always welcome motivated candidates.
